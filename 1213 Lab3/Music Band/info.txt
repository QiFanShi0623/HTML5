<p>Linkin Park is
  an American rock
  band from Agoura Hills,
  California. Formed in 1996,
  the band rose to international <br>fame with their debut album
  Hybrid Theory (2000), which was certified Diamond by the RIAA in 2005,
  and multi-Platinum in several other countries. Their following studio album
  Meteora continued the band's success, topping the Billboard <br>200 album chart in 2003,
  and was followed by extensive touring and charity work.</p>
